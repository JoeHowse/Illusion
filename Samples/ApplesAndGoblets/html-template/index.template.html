What? This augmented reality (AR) demo uses Illusion SDK, which integrates the rendering functionality of Alternativa3D with the barcode and image tracking functionality of flare*tracker and flare*nft.

How to? Download and print these barcodes and images. Hold any of the prints in front of your webcam to see a 3D model superimposed atop it. Touch the center of any image (not a barcode, sorry) to start/stop rotating the 3D model. Try multiple prints at once.

How fast? Press the spacebar to show/hide the Alternativa3D profiling diagram. (First, you might need to click the video window in order to give it keyboard focus.) Press 'p' to pause/unpause the tracker and 3D rendering for a clearer impression of their latency relative to the video background.

Who? I am Joe Howse (josephhowse@nummist.com), the author of Illusion SDK.
